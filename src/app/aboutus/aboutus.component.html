About Us

World is witnessing the coming water crises and its shortage. whether it is human, animals, or plants, everyone is completely depending on fresh and potable water. How will our planet manage to nourish us all? The answer is simple, and it lies in our hands. Water Saviours team took the initiative to save water. We are initially focusing on the development of prototype to query geolocations of sustainable water sources.

We provide call for code challenge solution to help individual worldwide by providing needed information based on their geolocation. This time we focus on WATER.

  • We developed a prototype which provides water sources in nearby areas
  • Type of water resources and their location
  • Use map to show the resources
  • Send this information to your mobile

Water Saviours are located in Bangalore (India) and developed the call for code challenge using latest technology such as Angular ({{ver}}) as front-end and Node JS as back-end. We deployed our solution on IBM cloud to make it globally available.
